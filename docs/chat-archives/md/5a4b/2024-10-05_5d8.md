1. Circle (○): p, t, k
2. Square (□): s, m, n
3. Triangle (△): l, j, w

Modifications:
- One dot inside: first letter of each group (p, s, l)
- Two dots inside: second letter (t, m, j)
- Line inside: third letter (k, n, w)

Vowels:
- Horizontal line (-): a
- Vertical line (|): i
- Diagonal line (/): u
